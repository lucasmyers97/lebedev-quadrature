Introduction

Because Lebedev quadrature is specifically designed to integrate polynomials up to a particular order, we can check for bugs in this implementation by checking the quadrature results against analytic solutions to integrals of monomials around the sphere. This is essentially what the test programs do, in addition to testing the lebedev::QuadraturePoints member function's ability to read in external functions to integrate.
